Jamie Marsella, PhD

I am a historian of medicine and a Postdoctoral Research Scholar at the Institute for Religion, Culture and Public Life at Columbia University. My work examines the role of domestic science, religion, and eugenic thought and practice in Progressive Era public health and child welfare.

I hold a PhD from Harvard University, an MA from the University of Chicago, and a BA from the University of Portland.
